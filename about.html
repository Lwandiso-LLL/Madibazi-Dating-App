About the Developer

Hi! I’m Lwandiso Nunu, the creator and developer of the Madibaz (temporary) Dating web application — a platform designed exclusively for students of Nelson Mandela University and other verified tertiary students.

I built this platform because I realized something: on campus, people want to connect, make friends, or even find love — but it’s not always easy. Some students are shy, others are busy, and many simply don’t know where to start.

Madibaz Dating exists to solve that problem. It provides a safe, university-only environment where students can match, have meaningful conversations, and meet people who understand campus life.

The vision is to grow an authentic, respectful, and fun student community where connections aren’t forced — they happen naturally.

Thank you for viewing this project and supporting its journey. This is only the beginning.
